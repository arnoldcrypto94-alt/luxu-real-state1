# Product Guidelines: LuxeEstate

## 1. Prose & Tone

The voice of the LuxeEstate platform must be **Elegant & Sophisticated**. All written content, from property descriptions to user interface text, should reflect prestige, quality, and exclusivity. The language should be refined, clear, and professional, reinforcing the luxury status of the brand and the properties it represents.

## 2. Visual Style

The visual aesthetic is **Minimalist & Clean**. The design should prioritize usability and let the high-quality property imagery speak for itself.

*   **Layout:** Emphasize generous white space and clean lines.
*   **Typography:** Utilize the primary fonts (Inter, Geist) to create a strong, legible typographic hierarchy.
*   **Focus:** The design should serve the content, ensuring that property visuals and key information are the central focus.

## 3. User Experience (UX) Principles

The user journey is paramount. The following principles must guide all design and development decisions:

*   **Simplicity & Ease of Use:** Interactions should be intuitive and straightforward. Reduce complexity at every turn to ensure a frictionless experience for all users, from first-time visitors to seasoned agents.
*   **Seamless & Integrated Journey:** The user experience must be consistent and connected across the entire platform. The journey from property discovery and search to scheduling visits and closing should feel like a single, cohesive process.
*   **Personalization & Customization:** Empower users by allowing them to tailor the experience to their needs. This includes features like saved searches, personalized dashboards, and curated recommendations.

## 4. Branding

Strict adherence to the brand identity is critical for maintaining a cohesive and premium feel.

*   **Color Palette:** The established color palette is not a suggestion; it is a strict requirement.
    *   `primary`: `#06f9d0`
    *   `mosque` (Active States): `#006655`
    *   `background-light`: `#EEF6F6`
    *   `background-dark`: `#0f231f`
    *   `nordic-dark`: `#19322F`
*   **Imagery:** The platform's reputation hinges on the quality of its visuals. All property photography and videography must be professional, high-resolution, and compelling.
*   **Iconography:** To ensure visual consistency, all icons used throughout the application must be from the **Material Symbols** library.
